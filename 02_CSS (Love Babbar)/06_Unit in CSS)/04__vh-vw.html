vh:- Relative to 1% of the height of the viewport*

For example, if the viewport height is 1000 pixels, then 1vh would be equal to 10 pixels (since 1% of 1000 is 10).

vw:- Relative to 1% of the width of the viewport*

vw: This unit represents 1% of the viewport width. Similarly, if the viewport width is 1000 pixels, then 1vw would be equal to 10 pixels

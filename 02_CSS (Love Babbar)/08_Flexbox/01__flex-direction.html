Flexbox:- It is one dimensional layout for arranging items in rows & columns.

main-axes (horizontal:- x-axis) & cross-axes (vertically:- Y-axis)

    flexbox direction

  • flex-direction: row; (default)
    [main axis:- row (from left to right) i.e:- horizontally] & [cross axis:- (top to bottom) i.e:- vertically]
  • flex-direction: row-reverse;
    [main axis:- row (from right to left) i.e:- horizontally]
  • flex-direction: column;
    [main axis:- column (from Top to bottom) i.e:- vertically]
  • flex-direction: column-reverse;
    [main axis:- column (from bottom to Top) i.e:- vertically]

The box-shadow CSS property adds shadow effects around an element's frame.
A box shadow is described by X and Y offsets relative to the element, blur and spread radius, and color.

  • h-offset / x-offset => The horizontal offset of the shadow. A positive value puts the shadow on the right side of the box, a negative value puts the shadow on the left side of the box
  • v-offset / y-offset => The vertical offset of the shadow. A positive value puts the shadow below the box, a negative value puts the shadow above the box

Shadow property

box-shadow: (x-offset/h-offset) | (y-offset/v-offset) | color
